REDUCE

'Reduce' is all about creating less waste,
which is a great way to benefit the environment
A few things which be included are:
-Buying only neccessary items
-Use reusable materials instead of plastic
-Consuming products with less packaging
-Donating used items instead of throwing them
-Repair, dont discard

REUSE

'Reuse' is all about finding new ways
to use trash so that we don't have to
throw it away. Reusing materials decreases
waste and pollution as it reduces the
need for raw materials and hence,saving
forest and water supplies. An example of
this process can be donating of household
items.

RECYCLE

'Recycling' is all about converting waste, that would normally
be thrown away, into usable materials and products. This reduces pollution and keeps the environemnt
clean. An example can be when glass bottles are returned and then
later processed into new glass products. To know about more about
